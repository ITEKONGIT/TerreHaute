Terre Haute

A lightweight web tool for reverse engineering and rebuilding Android APKs locally.

What It Does

Terre Haute empowers developers, security researchers, and Android enthusiasts to:

  • Decompile APKs into human-readable Smali and XML using Apktool.
  • Rebuild modified APKs with precision.
  • Sign APKs with custom or debug keystores using jarsigner.
  • Keep all operations local—no data ever leaves your machine.

Why It Exists

Many APK tools rely on online servers, risking privacy and security. Terre Haute runs entirely offline, giving you full control and confidentiality over your files. It’s built for those who demand trust and autonomy in their tools.

Who It’s For

  • Penetration testers & ethical hackers
  • Android developers
  • Reverse engineers
  • Malware analysts
  • Privacy-conscious power users

Features

  • APK decompilation using Apktool
  • APK recompilation with detailed error logging
  • APK signing with SHA1withRSA
  • Built-in keystore support (debug and custom)
  • No cloud storage—everything stays local
  • Cyberpunk web interface powered by Flask and Celery
  • Secure uploads with rate limiting and authentication
